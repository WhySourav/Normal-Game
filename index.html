🌍 RUN - Save Yourself! -

🎯 you are the Runner

Collect green Oxygen, avoid red pollution — survive as long as you can.

Use arrow keys or WASD Collect: ⚡ +10 | Hit: Game Over

How to play

Move: ← → ↑ ↓ or A D W S
Fullscreen on Play
Respawn button appears on death
background music will start when you click play

This game is based on polluton and oxygen i tried to make it responsive and colorfull . The canvas will scale in fullscreen and on resize. The CSS background uses lightweight layered gradients and transforms (no WebGL) so it works across browsers while trying to give a 3D-like depth.
